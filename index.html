GOSH: Global Online Support Hub

The Global Online Support Hub (GOSH) was designed as an alternative community resource to connect patients and their caregivers with others who find themselves in a similar situation.


HIGHLIGHTS

Features you love

Patient Resources

DefeatMSA provided a directory to assist in your search for a specialist. Staying connected to others with similar conditions can aid in finding the most appropriate treatment options.

Video Chat Support Groups

GOSH users and family can attend group sessions via video chat. Volunteer moderators help facilitate the group conversation. volunteer

Privacy

We take extraordinary precautions to ensure that your data remains private. Our security layers and features forbid individuals to search posts or messages. more info

Benefits of Video Chat Support Groups

  • Improve coping skills and managing stress.

  • A convenient place for patients and caregivers to compare notes on conditions.

  • Get information about your local health professionals and treatment options.
